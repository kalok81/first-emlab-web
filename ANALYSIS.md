# Brand Analysis: First Embroidery 初見

## Brand Info
- **Name:** First Embroidery 初見 | Embroidery Workshop HK
- **Bio (OCR):** 
  Based in Hong Kong | Hand embroidery
  學會刺繡，也學會過溫暖的日子。
  工作室地點：觀塘興業街
  工作坊 · 訂製 · 材料包
- **Category:** Handcraft Studio / Embroidery Education

## Visual Identity
- **Vibe:** Handmade, Warm, Japanese Zakka, Minimalist, Textured.
- **Color Palette (Hex):**
  - `#FDFBF8` (Ivory White) - Primary background, represents natural linen/fabric.
  - `#C6A682` (Warm Oak) - Secondary color, inspired by wooden embroidery hoops.
  - `#4A3728` (Deep Espresso) - Main text color, provides soft contrast.
  - `#D66D52` (Muted Terracotta) - Accent color for call-to-action or highlights.
- **Font Suggestion:**
  - **Headings:** *Noto Serif TC* (Elegant, traditional, fits the "First Sight" theme).
  - **Body:** *Noto Sans TC* or *Klee One* (Clean, legible, with a slight handwritten feel).

## Site Architecture
1. **Hero Section:** Full-width high-res image of a detailed embroidery piece (e.g., the floral or portrait work). Overlay text: "學會刺繡，也學會過溫暖的日子".
2. **About 初見:** Narrative about the therapeutic nature of embroidery and the studio's origin in Kwun Tong.
3. **Workshop (Workshops):** A clean grid showing different courses:
   - Level 1: Basic Stitches
   - Level 2: Creative Patterns
   - Special: Pet Embroidery / Portrait Customization
4. **Gallery (作品集):** A masonry or square grid layout mimicking the Instagram feed, showcasing the diversity of work (animals, people, flowers).
5. **Kits & Custom (材料包與訂製):** Section highlighting DIY kits for home practice and custom gift services.
6. **Visit Us (聯絡我們):** Studio address in Kwun Tong with a minimalist map and booking links.
